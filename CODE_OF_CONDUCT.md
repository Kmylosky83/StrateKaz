# Code of Conduct

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the StrateKaz project.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all communications.
- Assume good faith and be constructive when giving feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, hate speech, derogatory or exclusionary comments, and threats are not tolerated.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to `opensource@stratekaz.com`. Reports will be handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take action, including removal from the project, at their discretion to stop or prevent unacceptable behavior.

---

For incidents involving legal or safety concerns, contact the maintainers immediately via the address above.
